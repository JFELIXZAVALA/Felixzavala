About the Artist

I'm a professional graphic designer currently working out of the Dallas-Fort Worth Metroplex. In my spare time I like to train brazilian jiu-jitsu and working on my designs. I am finishing up my degree in Graphic Design and Media Arts with a concentration in 3D Modeling from Southern New Hampshire University and hope to be done by the end of 2022.


My genuine passion for art in all its forms fuels me to pursue improvement in my craft. I'm always looking for new opportunities to work on creative projects with talented individuals. If you're interested in collaborating, please send me an email to jfelixzavala@yahoo.com
