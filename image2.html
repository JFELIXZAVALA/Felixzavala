Handley Hope Center Logo 🙏

Transforming lives in East Fort Worth


       


What is it

I was hired to create a logo for a newly formed outreach center.

  • Gathered input from the client to get an understanding of what they were envisioning
  • I created some concept ideas for the client to revise. I was asked to combine some ideas for the final concept.
  • Created the final design in Adobe Illustrator.

Insight into my vision

I wanted the logo to express Hope. I was intentional in using the color yellow because it represents the color of hope and optimisim.
