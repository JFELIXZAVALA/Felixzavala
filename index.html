A little about me...

Based, out of the Dallas-Fort Worth area I approach every project with passion and determination. I am well versed with Adobe design software on both PC and Mac platforms. My goals are to help companies grow by creating visual and stunning graphics that can help promote the business through the use of literature and social media. I also have experience with product photography and I am a certified photographic retoucher. I have spent many years working in a corporate enviroment and I have a lot of experience managing standard office software such Microsoft Outlook, Excel, Word, and creating Powerpoint presentations.
