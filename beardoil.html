Beard Oil 🧔

Smooth like butter


       

What is it

This is a label that I created for the beard oil I make. I mainly make it for myself but I have been known to gift it to a few other beardsmen.

What I did

  • I digitally created the design
  • Placed the final concept on several different mock-ups
  • Converted the final design to print with a transparent background and printed on natural colored labels

Insight into my vision

I wanted to create something out of a western cowboy film.
