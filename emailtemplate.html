How Important is Cardio?

Cardio is an important part of any fitness routine. But, there are some people who think that they should do it everyday. The truth is, you don't have to do cardio every day. You can do it as often as you want, but try not to overdo it.

The benefits of cardio workouts are numerous and include improved heart health, weight loss and increased energy levels. If you're someone who loves doing cardio workouts then go ahead and do them every day if you want to!
